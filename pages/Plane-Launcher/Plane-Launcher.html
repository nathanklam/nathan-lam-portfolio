Background

From 2022 to 2024, I was the lead 3D-designer and fabricator for Pasadena High School's robotics team: IFC #15157. The team participates in a competition known as the FIRST Tech Challenge. For the competitition in 2023 to 2024, one of the objectives was to launch a paper airplane.
